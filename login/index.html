How It Works:

1

2

Claim Generation

Create a unique DNA fingerprint from your VCF file (processed locally on your device, not uploaded)

3

Verification

Register your claim via Story Protocol using GenoBank.io's patented BioNFT™ technology

4

Service Request

Request one of our annotation packages for your VCF file

Privacy Commitment:

Your VCF file stays securely on your device at all times. GenoBank.io never accesses or uploads your complete genetic data unless you explicitly choose to store your VCF file safely in one of our encrypted GenoVaults. Doing so allows you to interact privately and securely with our suite of privacy-preserving applications.

For standard use, we only generate a cryptographic fingerprint derived from 96 carefully selected genetic markers. This fingerprint creates a unique identifier that protects your privacy while enabling secure verification of your claim.
